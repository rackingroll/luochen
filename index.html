Biography

Hi, I am a Master candidate in College of Computer Science, Jilin University, supervised by Prof. Zhe Wang and Prof. Wei Pang. I received his bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Jilin University, Changchun, China, in 2012.

During undergrad study, I worked as a developer intern in Sina Weibo and MIUI Group at Xiaomi . During my master study, he worked as a research intern in Software Analytics Group at Microsoft Research Asia, Beijing, China. His current research interests include data mining and machine learning.
